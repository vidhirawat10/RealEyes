What is Face Recognition?

Face recognition is a technology that identifies or verifies a person's identity by analyzing and comparing their facial features.

What is Face Swapping?

Face swapping is the process of replacing one person's face with another's in photos or videos. This can be done for various reasons, including entertainment, art, or sometimes for purposes like creating deep fake content.

How is Face Swapped Video Detected?

Face-swapped videos are detected by looking for signs like mismatched facial features, digital artifacts, and inconsistencies in lighting or shadows. AI and machine learning help spot these anomalies.

Why we Made This Web App?

We created the RealEyes web app to address the growing problem of deep fake videos, especially those with face-swapping. Deep Fakes can be used to spread misinformation and damage reputations.
